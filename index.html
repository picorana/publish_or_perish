Hello! This is Publish or Perish, a game about surviving and thriving in academia.
I spend way too long looking at my own Scholar page, so I made a game about it (:

This is an incremental game - the whole point is watching numbers go up. In a way, the pleasure of watching numbers go up in an incremental game is similar to the pleasure of watching the number of citations go up over the years. Incremental games have seen some recent interest in academia, such as in this [1] CHI 2018 paper.

How to play:
  • Click "write" next to a paper to write!
  • Completed papers get cited. Different papers have different levels of success, so some papers get cited more often than others. Citations give you money. Use money to buy upgrades.
  • Coauthors and PhD students help you write papers. Get coauthors to have papers write themselves.
  • H-index is measured as N papers having at least N citations. To have h-index 5, you need at least 5 papers with at least 5 citations. To get h-index 10, you need at least 10 papers with at least 10 citations.
  • Get to h-index 100!
